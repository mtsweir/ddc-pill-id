512 (Acetaminophen and Oxycodone 325 mg / 5 mg)

Pill imprint 512 has been identified as Acetaminophen and oxycodone hydrochloride 325 mg / 5 mg.

Acetaminophen/oxycodone is used in the treatment of chronic pain; pain and belongs to the drug class narcotic analgesic combinations. Risk cannot be ruled out during pregnancy. Acetaminophen/oxycodone 325 mg / 5 mg has a high potential for abuse. The drug has a currently accepted medical use in treatment in the United States or a currently accepted medical use with severe restrictions. Abuse of the drug may lead to severe psychological or physical dependence.
